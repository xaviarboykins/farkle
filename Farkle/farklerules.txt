Farkle Rules

Farkle is a multiplayer dice game for 2 or more players.
The goal is to reach 10,000 points first.

Scoring:
- A 1 is worth 100 points.
- A 5 is worth 50 points.
- Three of a kind is worth 100 times the face value.
- Three 1s are worth 1,000 points.

How to Play:
- A player rolls all six dice.
- Any scoring dice can be set aside.
- The player may stop and keep the points from that turn, or keep rolling the remaining dice.
- If no scoring dice appear, that is a Farkle.
- If a Farkle happens, the player loses all points from that turn.
- If all six dice become scoring dice, the player must roll all six again at least once.

Entering the Game:
- A player must score at least 1,000 points in one turn before they can officially enter the game.
- After entering, the player may stop on any future turn whenever they want.

Winning:
- The first player to reach 10,000 points triggers the final round.
- All other players get one last turn to beat that score.
- If nobody beats it, that player wins.
- If another player beats it, the new high scorer wins.
